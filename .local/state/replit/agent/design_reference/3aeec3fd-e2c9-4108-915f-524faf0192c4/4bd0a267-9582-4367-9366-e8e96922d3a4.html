About UniHealth

UniHealth is the Unified Patient Medical Record system designed to connect patients and doctors seamlessly. With UniHealth, patients own their medical history, while doctors gain reliable insights for better decisions.

For Patients

  • Complete control over your medical data
  • Portable records across all healthcare providers
  • Smart health reminders and insights
  • Secure access from anywhere

For Doctors

  • Instant access to complete patient history
  • Reduced medical errors and duplicated tests
  • Faster diagnosis and treatment workflows
  • Seamless collaboration with specialists
